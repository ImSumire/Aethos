Installation Guide

Download Forge Installer
  • Visit the official Forge website.
  • Select the 1.19.2 version (shortcut)
  • Download the latest or recommended

Run Forge Installer
  • Once the installer is downloaded, locate the file in your downloads folder or wherever you saved it.
  • Double-click the installer file to launch it. If you are on Linux : `chmod +x forge-1.19.2-43.3.8-installer.jar`
  • Select "Install client" and then click "OK". Forge will begin installing.

Launch Minecraft with Forge
  • Open the Minecraft Launcher.
  • In the bottom-left corner, click on the drop-down menu next to the "Play" button.
  • Select "Forge" from the list of available installations.
  • Click the "Play" button to launch Minecraft with Forge.

Verify Forge Installation
  • Once Minecraft has launched, check the bottom-left corner of the main menu screen.
  • You should see the Forge version displayed, confirming that Forge has been successfully installed.

Install Mods
  • Download the modpack you want to install in regards to your computer's power.
  • Windows : Press WINDOWS + R simultaneously and type %appdata% in the window and open the .minecraft directory.
  • Linux : Open your Home directory, press CTRL + H and open the .minecraft directory.
  • In your Minecraft directory, you should see a folder named "mods." If it doesn't exist, create one.
  • Drag and drop the modpack into the "mods" folder and extract the zip.
  • Close the Minecraft Launcher if it's open, then relaunch Minecraft with Forge.

It's done!

Why 3 modpacks ?

Creating light, medium, and extreme modpacks for your Minecraft modpack is crucial for catering to a diverse player base with varying preferences and capabilities. The light modpack offers a streamlined experience suitable for players with lower-end hardware or those seeking a vanilla-like gameplay with minor enhancements. On the other hand, the medium modpack strikes a balance between performance and content, providing a more immersive experience with a broader range of mods for players with moderate hardware capabilities. Lastly, the extreme modpack caters to enthusiasts and players with high-end machines, delivering a comprehensive and challenging gameplay experience with an extensive selection of mods, pushing the boundaries of what Minecraft can offer. Offering these three tiers ensures inclusivity, allowing players of all preferences and hardware capabilities to enjoy your modpack to its fullest potential.
